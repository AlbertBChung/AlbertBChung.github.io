Showerfy

Autumn '16
An Android application built with team of five during Cornell's BigRed//Hacks 36 hour Hackathon.
Used the Spotify SDK to build a music player app.
The user's goal is to limit their shower time to the duration of two selected songs to save water!
User is rewarded or penalized with in-app points based on time difference and can share results on social media.
App is no longer supported by developers.

Collision Defense

Summer '16
A 2D game in Java.
The objective is to use a cannon to hit bombs on the ground to send them in a trajectory into the emeny slime monsters. The slimes slowly approach to attack the player's base.
The game was embedded onto HTML as a JApplet and hosted on a Apache Server on Linux.
Javascript, PHP, and XML to obtain user's score, append it to XML file, and display top scoreboard of real players.
